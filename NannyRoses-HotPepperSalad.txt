Nanny Rosie's Hot Pepper Salad

Everything Diced.                                                 
1. Large jar sweet peppers
1. Large jar hot peppers
2. Sticks of Celery
1 - 14oz. Jar of Spanish olives
1. Jar of pepper salad
3 or more cloves of fresh garlic. Also diced.
Add Olive Oil to taste. 
Pinch of Oregano.   
Can add hot fresh peppers if you like.  

Put in jars in fridge. Will keep a long time.
